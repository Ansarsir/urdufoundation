Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

My child has never learned Urdu — is that okay? +

Absolutely! Our Little Beginners course starts from the very first letter. No prior knowledge needed at all.

What device does my child need? +

Any smartphone, tablet or laptop with a camera and microphone. We use Zoom which is free to download.

How many students per batch? +

Maximum 6–8 students per batch so every child gets personal attention and the teacher tracks each student's progress.

What are the class timings? +

We offer morning, evening, and weekend batches to suit different timezones — India, UK, USA, Canada and Gulf. Your exact timing is discussed and confirmed during the free demo class so it fits your schedule perfectly.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes — no lock-in contracts. Cancel before the next billing cycle with no questions asked. We are confident you won't want to! 😊

Do you teach students outside India? +

Yes! We have dedicated weekend batches for UK, USA, Canada and Middle East. International payments accepted securely online.

What if my child misses a class? +

Missed classes can be made up in the next available slot. We also share class notes on WhatsApp so your child never falls behind.
